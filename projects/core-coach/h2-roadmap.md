# Coach H2 Roadmap

H2 is about expanding Coach across Culture Amp — with our proposed Coach Playbook supplemented by foundational initiatives that focus on large-scale adoption.

---

## Confidence key

| Label | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Committed | Scoped, resourced, in delivery |
| High confidence | Strong intent, clear path |
| Early signal | Directionally right, needs validation |
| Hypothesis | Worth exploring, low signal so far |
| Placeholder | Not yet defined |

| Label | Sprints | Duration |
|---|---|---|

| Medium | 2 | ~1 month |

## The Spine: Path to CA-wide Coach

The core sequenced path. Each initiative is a prerequisite for the next.

| Initiative | Start | Confidence | Effort | Drives | Description |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Routing — MSS slice | Q3.0 | Committed | Medium | Engagement | Captures premium insights for our high-value leaders-of-scale and admin segment. Initial focus: retention insights. |
| Unified Sessions + Routing | Q3.0 | Committed | Medium | Reach | Consolidates Coach sessions into a single history view — making Coach feel like one product; solves navigation when Coach is open and lays the foundation for CA-wide Coach. |
| Page Fluency | Q3.3 | Early signal | Medium | Engagement | Makes Coach native to the page — aware of what's on screen, able to write directly into forms, and responsive to what the user is doing. Drives engagement by replacing copy-paste with in-page action. |
| CA-wide Coach | Q4.0 | High confidence | Medium | Reach | Extends Coach beyond its current pages to a persistent, platform-wide presence — increasing reach by putting Coach in front of users wherever they are in Culture Amp. |

---

## The Bets: Value-Add Initiatives

Orbiting the spine. More exploratory — each has a distinct adoption hypothesis.

| Initiative | Start | Confidence | Effort | Drives | Description |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Improved Observability | Q3.0 | High confidence | Medium | Foundation | Builds the signal layer Coach currently lacks — understanding what users ask, how conversations resolve, and what they think. Creates new feedback channels and drives better product decisions. |
| Customer Knowledge Base | Q3.3 | Early signal | Medium | Engagement | Gives admins the ability to ground Coach in their organisation's values, priorities and frameworks — with the hypothesis that greater admin control drives internal advocacy and, in turn, usage. |
| Memory | Q4.0 | Hypothesis | Medium | Engagement | Gives Coach the ability to retain and build on what it knows about a user across sessions — making replies more personally relevant over time and increasing the reason to return. |
| Coach via MCP | Q4.0 | Early signal | Medium | Reach (off-platform) | Makes Coach — and CA data — accessible to other assistants via MCP, enabling customers to query CA externally with the safety of Coach as an interpretive layer. Drives reach by meeting users where they already work, off-platform. |
| Charts & Graphs | Q4.2 | Placeholder | Medium | TBD | — |
| Extending Explainability | Q4.4 | Placeholder | Medium | TBD | — |

## Not in H2

| Initiative | Reason |
|---|---|
| Vector Storage & Search | Core infrastructure work that doesn't directly unlock user-facing value in H2 — will be revisited when retrieval quality becomes a bottleneck. |
| Performance & Cost Optimisation | Important, but unlikely to move adoption metrics at current scale — and cost is not yet prohibitive. Deferred in favour of higher-impact initiatives. |
| Subgraph Evolution | Insufficiently defined to commit to — needs scoping before it can be prioritised. |
