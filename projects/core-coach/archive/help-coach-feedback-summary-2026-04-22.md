# #help-coach-feedback Summary — Jan to Apr 2026

## Positive Highlights

- **Strong praise from enterprise customers**: Laing O'Rourke (APAC) praised the seamless enablement and product quality. University of Sydney, APM's Global CPO, and Coles (2,000+ leaders rollout planned May 2026) all reported strong positive sentiment.
- **Coach in Perform for manager reviews is a standout**: Multiple internal users (Ramy Saad, Jay Linsell, Chris Pickford) called it a "game changer" — particularly for drafting manager reviews from messy notes. Team reaction was enthusiastic (26 reactions on one post).
- **Anytime feedback link to Coach** appreciated — opens in new tab without losing context.

---

## Key Themes — Issues & Requests

### 1. Data Access & Boundaries (most recurring)
- Coach **doesn't respect review cycle date boundaries** — pulls feedback from previous cycles and only catches it if prompted (multiple posts from Prakriti Mateti)
- Coach **can't access historical manager reviews**, only peer/self-reflection/ratings data (Prakriti Mateti, Doug English)
- Coach **can't access peer feedback for some direct reports** (Louis Simoneau and others)
- Coach **can't pull certain demographic dimensions** e.g. Job Title from heatmap data (Fayssal Merheb)

### 2. Data Accuracy / Hallucination
- Coach **claims no supporting quotes exist when they do** (Prakriti Mateti)
- **Demographic comparisons are inaccurate** — Coach incorrectly ranked engagement by department vs. what the heatmap shows (multiple customers via Tim Seemuth, Bionic)
- **Key themes don't update based on applied filters/views** — always returns company-wide results regardless of demographic filter applied

### 3. UX & Navigation
- **Can't pin the highlights/citations panel** — users have to scroll back to the top repeatedly during a session (Mindy Eiermann)
- **Session timestamps missing** — hard to navigate previous conversations without dates (Prakriti Mateti)
- **Historical chats inaccessible** — error when clicking into previous sessions (Mads Hallett, Apr 2026)
- **Coach button causes confusion** when General Coach is restricted to a pilot group — employees see the button but hit a 404/blank page (Ada Lisowska re: Pipedrive; also reported in demo environments)
- **Feedback names/attribution shown in output** is confusing — users want consolidated output without listing individual reviewers (Katharina Matsui)
- **Pasted text treated as snippets unexpectedly** (Jo Montanari)
- **Can't select/copy text from Coach output** (Ashley Cambrell)

### 4. Contextual Understanding
- Customers (e.g. Datadog via Tim Seemuth) flagged **lack of role/org context** — Coach doesn't know enough about who the person is or their team structure
- Coach **uses generic frameworks** (e.g. SBI model) without grounding them in the user's actual situation — feels canned (Ananda Rodziewicz)
- Customer at lastminute.com (Perform EAP): **session framing and quality of Coach responses** was a concern

### 5. Access & Permissions
- **Super user / admin access to Coach** in pilot groups has been a recurring issue — long-running thread from Feb 2026 with a fix still in progress as of April 2026
- **Admin reporting is limited** — RPM International couldn't pull a list of active Coach users to drive internal adoption (Rebecca Nottingham)

### 6. Integrations & Scope
- Request for **MS Teams integration** for Coach (Georgie Cameron, customer ask)
- Request to include **Anytime Feedback and Goals** in Coach in Perform (Ada Lisowska)
- Request to better integrate **Suggested Insights (SI) with Coach** — flagged as a near-term roadmap item by Suyin Kee
- Request to add **Coach best practices to PS Masterclass content** to drive adoption (Tim Seemuth)

### 7. Quantitative Metrics
- **No post-AI Coach ROI metrics available** — Devshree Bhatt flagged a need for quantitative success measures for Engage/Perform (time to create action plan, quality of output) to update the pre-AI Forrester stat
