:microphone: Voice — Rollout Plan

Hi all, with ElevenLabs signed, we wanted to share an update Voice release timeline:

**:vertical_traffic_light: Timeline at a Glance**
- **May 4** — ElevenLabs deal signed ✅
- **May 19** — Customer Zero release
- **Jun 2** — Beta begins (10%)
- **Early Jun** — Sales Enablement
- **Jun 9** — 30% rollout
- **Jun 16** — 100% rollout

**:dart: Feature scope**
- **Dictation mode** — voice-to-text input, available across all Coaches
- **Conversation mode** — free back-and-forward spoken conversation, General Coach only at launch
- Conversation mode expansion to other Coaches to be evaluated post-Beta based on data

**:busts_in_silhouette: Phase 1: Customer Zero (May 19 →)**
Two tracks running in parallel:
- **Company-wide** — Voice enabled for all of CZ; feedback via #help-coach-feedback and Coach Camp
- **Focused qualitative panel** — ~10 Campers guided by UXR (Darshana), split across two shortlisted voices. One week of testing, one week to implement feedback before Beta
Gate to Beta: final voice selected; no P0/P1 bugs; security items done; consent flow live; analytics live; OKTA implemented.

**:rocket: Phase 2: Voice Beta — Graduated Rollout (Jun 2 →)**
- **Jun 2:** 10% of accounts
- **Jun 9:** 30% of accounts
- **Jun 16:** 100% of accounts
- Labelled as "Beta" to preserve flexibility on scope and pricing

**:microphone: Voice selection**
Recommendation is to launch with a single voice from the PSX shortlist. The CZ panel will test both candidates; with a 'final' Voice ID to be recommended post CZ test. Longer-term options (user-selectable catalogue vs. custom/cloned Coach voice) deferred until we have more usage data.

**:key: A note on Sales Enablement**
Sales Enablement is slated for early June, timed to coincide with the wider rollout. We're finalising a proposed demo talk track for SDEs — Dictation is fully demo-able; Conversation mode is described rather than run live. Proposal is with Tom Lewis for sign-off.

Hope this makes sense. Please shout with any questions!
