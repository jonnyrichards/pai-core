:gong: Gong - Voice mode is now live in 100% Beta! :gong:

:raised_hands: What's new?
Voice is now rolled out to all customers as a Beta feature. Users can speak to Coach rather than type — via Dictation (voice-to-text, available across all Coaches) or Conversation mode (free back-and-forward spoken exchange, General Coach only). This gives us parity with competitors who have shipped voice features, creates a more efficient input method to Coach ('Let me braindump'!), and enables new Coach interactions like preparing for difficult conversations, as well as role-play.

:lovedata: The Details
- **Dictation mode** — voice-to-text input (available across all Coach modules
- **Conversation mode** — free, two-way spoken conversation with General Coach
- **Streaming audio with synchronised text** — see and hear responses in real time
- **Seamless mode switching** — Coach shifts between voice and text mid-reply
- **Interruption handling** — users can interrupt Coach mid-response
- **Latency** — average time-to-first-token for voice requests is 1–2s
- **Privacy** — no audio is ever stored; we're on ElevenLabs' enterprise Zero Retention tier

:dart: Why this matters?
- Closes the competitive gap with Lattice, who market voice as a differentiator
- Removes a friction in Coach conversations — typing out long messages
- Managers are already using General Coach to prepare for a difficult 1:1s and workshop how to deliver feedback; Voice mode becomes a natural fit for these conversations

:chart_with_upwards_trend How's it going so far?
- We're seeing good early signal: in the past week (at 20% rollout), 4% of Coach users tried Dictation, and 75% of those interactions completed successfully. (Our target is 1-2% retained usage (ie. complete 3+ voice interactions).) We'll have a more complete read out in a month.

:woman-running::skin-tone-2: What's next?
- Voice will feature as part of the Perform bundle campaign alongside new Coach in Perform and Perform capabilities in late June and July
- We're assessing uptake and planning the next phase. Considerations: extending Conversation mode to other Coach modules (relevant as we bring Coach modules together for MSI), reducing latency in tool-heavy agents, UI improvements to signal 'response processing' more effectively
