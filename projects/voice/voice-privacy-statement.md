# Your Privacy with AI Coach Voice

Your voice conversations with AI Coach are private. No one else in your organization — including your manager or administrators — can see or hear your interactions.

Before your first session, we remind you that audio recordings will only be used to help Coach respond and won't be shared with your organization.

Voice audio is processed in real time and not stored. Neither Culture Amp nor our voice processing provider retains any recordings of your sessions, and your voice data is not used to train third party models.                           
                                                                                        
We prioritize safety and fairness in AI, with safeguards actively in place, and are committed to strengthening them over time.
