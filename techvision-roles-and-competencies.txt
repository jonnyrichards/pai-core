# Roles & Competencies
# 3 levels × 3 functions — SaaS software company

## Software Engineer

**Graduate**
- Writes clean code with guidance; learns the codebase incrementally
- Completes well-scoped tickets; asks good questions
- Understands basics of testing, version control, and CI/CD

**Mid**
- Delivers features end-to-end with minimal oversight
- Identifies and resolves bugs independently; reviews peers' code
- Contributes to technical design discussions; improves team practices

**Senior**
- Leads design of complex systems; sets technical direction for a squad
- Mentors junior engineers; raises the bar on code quality and architecture
- Partners cross-functionally to shape product and engineering trade-offs

---

## Product Manager

**Graduate**
- Supports discovery and delivery work; writes tickets and docs
- Synthesises user feedback and data with guidance
- Learns to prioritise and communicate trade-offs

**Mid**
- Owns a product area end-to-end; drives roadmap with clear rationale
- Runs discovery independently; defines success metrics
- Aligns engineering, design, and go-to-market on delivery

**Senior**
- Sets vision and strategy for a product domain
- Influences company-level prioritisation; manages stakeholder expectations
- Coaches PMs; drives cross-functional alignment at scale

---

## Product Marketing Manager

**Graduate**
- Supports launches, content, and competitive research
- Drafts messaging with guidance; maintains sales enablement materials
- Learns customer segments and ICP

**Mid**
- Owns go-to-market for features or segments independently
- Develops positioning and messaging; runs launch campaigns
- Partners with sales and CS to drive adoption and retention

**Senior**
- Defines category positioning and market narrative
- Leads major product launches; shapes pricing and packaging strategy
- Coaches PMMs; aligns marketing strategy with product and revenue goals
