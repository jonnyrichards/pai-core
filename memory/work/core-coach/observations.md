- 2026-04-30 [work, core-coach, meet-coach-modal, amplitude, ab-test]: Delivered collab session 'Whither the Meet Coach Modal'. Amplitude data showed poor conversion through the Meet Coach Modal (MCM) funnel: of users whose Side Panel opens (12.9% of Engagement Report Viewed), only 7.8% see the MCM, and of those just ~2% reach the Consent Page — though 90% of that 2% go on to view the Coach page, so intent is high once they get there. Critically, drop-off from Modal→Consent is far greater than Consent→Coach Page. By comparison, users who click Coach in the nav bar (a direct signal) reach Coach Page Viewed at ~75% — and General Coach has no MCM. Three hypotheses: (1) people aren't finding Engage Coach, not that it's less useful; (2) the MCM itself is a significant barrier (one part that looks like a button doesn't work); (3) many users aren't seeing the Side Panel because their screens are too narrow. Committed outcome: run an A|B test — remove the MCM and measure whether conversion to Coach Page Viewed increases. [work, core-coach, meet-coach-modal, amplitude, ab-test, conversion, ux]
